Team Rules

  • Clear Communication: We'll keep things open and honest, making sure everyone's thoughts and concerns are heard.
  • Stick to Deadlines: We respect deadlines and will do our part to keep the project on track.
  • Team Support: We'll help each other out and make sure no one feels stuck or overwhelmed.
  • Give and Take Feedback: We're here to grow, so we'll offer feedback constructively and take it in stride.
  • Respect Each Other's Time: We'll be mindful of everyone's time by keeping meetings efficient and responding promptly.
  • Stay Flexible and Keep Learning: We're all about learning new things and adapting when challenges come up.

Our goal is to do great work, support each other, and make the most of this experience to become better engineers and teammates.
